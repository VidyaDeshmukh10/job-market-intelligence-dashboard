## Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

# Overview
This project analyzes Data Analyst job postings from 2022–2025 to understand hiring trends, salary distribution, remote work opportunities, and demand across companies and locations.
The project follows a complete data analytics workflow—from data cleaning and exploration to SQL analysis and dashboard development in Power BI.


# Objectives
- Analyze hiring trends over time
- Compare salaries across experience levels
- Identify the companies with the highest hiring demand
- Explore geographic distribution of job opportunities
- Compare remote and on-site hiring


# Dataset
Metric -> Value
- Job Postings -> 62,000+
- Companies	-> 13,000+
- Locations	-> 978
- Time Period	-> 2022–2025

# Key Findings
- Mid-level positions account for the largest share of job postings.
- Lead roles have the highest average salaries.
- Upwork appears among the highest hiring companies in the dataset.
- Most salaries fall between $60,000 and $120,000.
- Remote opportunities represent a significant portion of total postings.
